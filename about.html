About

We build software tools that work.

ITO AI is a SaaS company focused on building AI-powered tools for automation, analytics, and digital productivity.

Our mission

We exist to make powerful AI technology accessible through simple, well-designed software tools. No complexity for complexity's sake — just sharp tools that solve real problems.

Every product we build follows the same philosophy: automate the tedious, surface the meaningful, and stay out of the way.

What we do

ITO AI develops and operates a SaaS platform that hosts multiple AI-powered software tools. Our products span automation, analytics, and digital productivity.

We are a software company. We build tools that help people and teams work more efficiently with the help of artificial intelligence.

How we build

Principles that guide us

These aren't buzzwords — they're the decisions we make every day.

C

Clarity over hype

We describe what our tools do in plain language. No inflated claims, no vague promises. If you can't understand it in 5 seconds, we haven't done our job.

U

Utility first

Every feature exists because it solves a real problem. We don't build features to pad a list — we build them because users need them.

T

Transparency

Clear pricing, clear terms, clear refund policies. We believe trust is built through transparency, not marketing.

The platform approach

Instead of building disconnected products, we built a unified platform. Every tool shares the same infrastructure: one account, one dashboard, one billing system.

This means less friction for users and a more reliable experience across every tool. As we add new products, your existing account grows with the platform.
