Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ITO AI respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you engage our services or visit our website.

1. Information we collect

Contact information: When you submit our contact form or reach out via email/WhatsApp, we collect your name, email address, and message content.

Payment information: Payment details are collected and processed securely by Yoco. We do not store your full card details on our servers.

Project data: Information you share with us during a project (business requirements, assets, content) is used solely to deliver the agreed-upon services.

Website analytics: We may collect basic usage data (pages visited, session duration) to improve our website. No invasive tracking.

2. How we use your data

  • To respond to your inquiries and communicate about projects
  • To deliver the digital services you've engaged us for
  • To process payments
  • To send project updates and important service communications
  • To improve our website and services

3. What we don't do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use your project data for purposes unrelated to your engagement

4. Data sharing

We only share your data in these limited circumstances:

  • Payment processing: With Yoco to process transactions
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or regulation
  • Service delivery: With trusted tools we use to build your systems (hosting, deployment), under strict data protection practices

5. Data security

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure storage for project files and client data
  • Access controls limiting who can view client information

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@itoai.com.

7. Data retention

We retain project data for 12 months after project completion for support purposes. Contact form submissions are retained for 6 months. You can request deletion at any time.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy with reasonable notice. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions: hello@itoai.com
