Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We want every client to be satisfied with the systems we build. This policy explains how refunds work for AIP Solutions services.

Before work begins

If you cancel a project before work has started, you are entitled to a full refund of any payments made.

During the project

  • If you cancel after work has begun, you will be charged only for the work completed to date
  • We will provide a summary of completed work and a prorated refund for the remaining balance
  • Any delivered work up to the cancellation point remains yours

After delivery

Once a project has been delivered and approved, the project fee is non-refundable. We include a post-launch support period (14 or 30 days depending on your plan) to address any issues.

Dispute resolution

If you are not satisfied with the delivered work, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will work with you to resolve the issue — this may include revisions, adjustments, or a partial refund at our discretion.

How to request a refund

  • Email hello@aipsolutions.co with your project details and reason for the request
  • Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method

Our commitment

We believe in fair, transparent billing. If you have any questions about charges, project scope, or your invoice, contact us at any time. We're here to help.

Contact

For refund requests or billing questions: hello@aipsolutions.co
