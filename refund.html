Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We want you to be satisfied with ITO AI's software tools. This policy explains how refunds work for all subscriptions on our platform.

7-Day refund guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your subscription, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase date. No questions asked.

How to request a refund

  • Email support@itoai.com with your account email and reason for the refund
  • Include your subscription details (plan name and purchase date)
  • Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method

Subscription renewals

For recurring monthly subscriptions:

  • You may cancel at any time to prevent future charges
  • Refunds for renewal charges can be requested within 48 hours of the renewal date
  • After 48 hours, the current billing period is non-refundable, but no further charges will occur after cancellation

What is not refundable

  • Accounts that have been terminated for violating our Terms of Service
  • Refund requests made after the 7-day window (initial purchase) or 48-hour window (renewals)

Our commitment

We believe in transparent, fair billing. If you have any questions about charges, billing, or your subscription, contact us at any time. We're here to help.

Contact

For refund requests or billing questions: support@itoai.com
